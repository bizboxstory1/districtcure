Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters to us. This policy explains what we collect, why, and how we protect it.

Last updated: May 28, 2026

Overview

District Cure Dispensary ("District Cure," "we," "us," or "our") operates districtcuredispensary.com (the "Site"). This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you visit the Site, place an order, or interact with us. By using the Site you agree to the practices described here.

Information We Collect

Information you provide

  • Order & contact details — name, phone number, email, and delivery address when you place an order or contact us.
  • Age verification — confirmation that you are 21 or older, and, where required, your medical cannabis registration and government-issued ID at the point of sale.
  • Messages — anything you send us through forms, email, or phone.

Information collected automatically

  • Usage data — pages viewed, device and browser type, and general location, collected through cookies and similar technologies to keep the Site working and improve it.

How We Use Your Information

  • To process and fulfill your orders, including delivery and pickup.
  • To verify you are of legal age (21+) and eligible to purchase.
  • To operate our loyalty program and apply promotions you choose to use.
  • To respond to your questions and provide customer support.
  • To improve our products, menu, and website experience.
  • To comply with DC law and the requirements of the Alcoholic Beverage and Cannabis Administration (ABCA).

How We Share Information

We do not sell your personal information. We share it only as needed:

  • Service providers — such as our ordering and payment platforms (e.g. our menu/checkout provider), delivery support, and analytics, who process data on our behalf.
  • Legal & compliance — when required by law, regulation, court order, or to comply with DC cannabis regulations.
  • Safety — to protect the rights, property, or safety of our customers, staff, or the public.

Cookies & Analytics

We use cookies and similar tools to remember your cart, keep the Site secure, and understand how visitors use the Site. You can control cookies through your browser settings; disabling them may affect some features.

Data Retention & Security

We keep personal information only as long as necessary to provide our services and meet legal and recordkeeping obligations, then dispose of it securely. We use reasonable administrative and technical safeguards to protect your information, though no method of transmission or storage is completely secure.

Your Choices

  • You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal information, subject to legal recordkeeping requirements.
  • You may opt out of marketing messages at any time.
  • To make a request, contact us using the details below.

Age Restriction

The Site and our products are intended only for adults 21 and older. We do not knowingly collect information from anyone under 21.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date above reflects the most recent version. Continued use of the Site after changes means you accept the updated policy.
