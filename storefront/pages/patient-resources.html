DC Residents — Self-Certify in Minutes

Since DC adopted self-certification, any DC resident 21 or older can register as a medical cannabis patient without seeing a doctor and without paying a fee.

Start your application online

Go to the DC Resident Medical Cannabis Patient Application on the ABCA (Alcoholic Beverage and Cannabis Administration) portal.

Self-certify (21+) or add a recommendation (under 21)

Adults 21+ simply self-certify — no practitioner required. Patients under 21 must include a recommendation from a licensed healthcare provider.

Get your temporary registration instantly

You'll receive a temporary digital registration valid for 30 days while your application is reviewed. That's enough to shop today.

Your full registration lasts up to two years

Once approved, your registration is valid for up to two years. Renewing later uses the same simple process.

✅
No fee for DC residents. You can also apply in person, by mail, or by email to ABCA.CannabisInfo@dc.gov if you prefer not to apply online.
Start My Application on ABCA → Opens the DC government portal (abca.dc.gov)

Visiting With a Medical Card

DC recognizes valid medical cannabis cards from other US states and territories. If your registration is current, you can shop at District Cure — just present your valid patient card and a government-issued photo ID at the door.

ℹ️
Purchase limit: Reciprocity patients may purchase up to 8 ounces of cannabis within a rolling 30-day period under current DC rules.
