Our Story

Georgia Ave's Cannabis Home

District Cure was built for our neighborhood — a calm, welcoming dispensary on Georgia Avenue near Howard University, where quality cannabis meets genuine, knowledgeable service.

Who We Are

District Cure Dispensary is a locally rooted, ABCA-licensed cannabis retailer in the heart of Washington DC. We opened to give our community a better way to shop — one that feels personal, informed, and free of the intimidation that can come with cannabis retail. Whether it's your first time or your hundredth, our team takes the time to understand what you're looking for and guide you to the right product.

We focus on a tightly curated selection rather than overwhelming shelves: small-batch flower, trusted local brands, fresh edibles, vapes, pre-rolls, concentrates, and wellness products — each chosen for quality and value.
